Resource and Referral Center

The goal of the Minneapolis College Resource and Referral Center is to connect our currently enrolled students with supportive community networks, resources and opportunities to ease obstacles interfering with their success. The Resource and Referral Center is part of Minneapolis College's Student Support Center, located in T.2300
